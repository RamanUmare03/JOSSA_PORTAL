About JoSAA 2024

JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) 2024 is responsible for seat allocation in various technical institutes across India including IITs, NITs, IIEST, IIITs, and other GFTIs. The aim is to ensure that the seat allocation process is streamlined and fair for all candidates.
